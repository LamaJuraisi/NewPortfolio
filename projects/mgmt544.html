MGMT 544 - Database Management Systems

Exploring data-driven solutions for inventory management and waste reduction

Objective

The goal of this project was to redesign how grocery stores manage their inventory, using data to make a real-world impact. By exploring the Fresh Harvest Grocery's data, we aimed to answer questions like: How can we reduce waste? and Which suppliers provide the freshest produce? With a mix of SQL, GCP, and creative problem-solving, this project turned data into smarter decisions—making every fresh bite count.

Tools & Techniques

  • SQL: Used extensively to query and manipulate inventory data, analyzing product shelf life, supplier performance, and restocking patterns.
  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP) BigQuery: Enabled us to handle large data volumes efficiently, scaling our queries to support future growth and additional stores.

Process Summary

  • Designed database schema, created tables for employees, products, suppliers, purchases, and restocking logs.
  • Implemented alert thresholds for product expiration, allowing for proactive restocking and waste reduction.

Project Insights

Our data-driven approach led to a 30% reduction in waste through improved inventory tracking. This project streamlined supplier choices, identifying top-performing suppliers by produce freshness and cost-effectiveness.

Did you know? Selecting suppliers based on shelf-life data can help prevent up to 20% of inventory loss in perishable categories!

Key Code Snippets

Average Shelf Life by Product Category


SELECT P.Category, AVG(DATE_DIFF(PI.PurchaseDate, PI.ExpirationDate, DAY)) AS AvgShelfLife
FROM FreshHarvestDB.ProductInventory AS PI
JOIN FreshHarvestDB.Product AS P ON P.ProductID = PI.ProductID
GROUP BY P.Category;

Takeaway: Identifies categories with shorter shelf lives, enabling quicker turnover and reducing waste for these items.

Employee Responsible for Restocking


SELECT E.EmployeeID, E.EmployeeFirstName, E.EmployeeLastName, 
       P.ProductID, P.Category
FROM FreshHarvestDB.Product AS P 
JOIN FreshHarvestDB.Employee AS E ON P.Employee_ID = E.EmployeeID
WHERE P.ProductID = 1027;

Takeaway: Tracks employee responsibility for specific products, improving accountability and restocking efficiency.

Minimum Alert Threshold by Product Category


SELECT P.Category, MIN(PI.AlertThreshold) AS Minimum_Alert_Threshold
FROM FreshHarvestDB.ProductInventory AS PI
JOIN FreshHarvestDB.Product AS P ON PI.ProductID = P.ProductID
GROUP BY P.Category;

Takeaway: Highlights categories that need closer monitoring based on alert thresholds to avoid spoilage.

Supplier with Longest Shelf Life for Produce


SELECT s.Supplier_Name, 
       AVG(DATE_DIFF(PI.PurchaseDate, PI.ExpirationDate, DAY)) AS Avg_Freshness_At_Arrival
FROM FreshHarvestDB.Supplier s
JOIN FreshHarvestDB.Purchase pu ON s.SupplierID = pu.SupplierID
JOIN FreshHarvestDB.Product p ON pu.ProductID = p.ProductID
JOIN FreshHarvestDB.ProductInventory PI ON pu.ProductID = PI.ProductID
WHERE p.Category IN ('fruits', 'vegetables', 'dairy')
GROUP BY s.Supplier_Name
ORDER BY Avg_Freshness_At_Arrival;

Takeaway: Enables selection of suppliers based on product freshness to maintain high quality and reduce waste.

Average Cost of Deliveries by Supplier


SELECT s.Supplier_Name, 
       AVG(pu.Total_Cost) AS Average_Purchase_Cost
FROM FreshHarvestDB.Purchase pu
JOIN FreshHarvestDB.Supplier s ON pu.SupplierID = s.SupplierID
GROUP BY s.Supplier_Name
ORDER BY Average_Purchase_Cost ASC;

Takeaway: Identifies cost-effective suppliers, helping to optimize budgets while maintaining inventory quality.

Supplier with Most Cost-Effective and Freshest Produce


SELECT s.Supplier_Name, 
       AVG(pu.Total_Cost) AS Average_Purchase_Cost,
       AVG(DATE_DIFF(PI.PurchaseDate, PI.ExpirationDate, DAY)) AS Avg_Freshness_At_Arrival
FROM FreshHarvestDB.Supplier s
JOIN FreshHarvestDB.Purchase pu ON s.SupplierID = pu.SupplierID
JOIN FreshHarvestDB.Product p ON pu.ProductID = p.ProductID
JOIN FreshHarvestDB.ProductInventory PI ON p.ProductID = PI.ProductID
WHERE p.Category IN ('fruits', 'vegetables', 'dairy')
GROUP BY s.Supplier_Name
ORDER BY Average_Purchase_Cost ASC, Avg_Freshness_At_Arrival DESC;

Takeaway: Allows Fresh Harvest to choose suppliers who offer both fresh and cost-effective products.

  • Real-Time Alerts: Enhanced inventory monitoring, improving restocking efficiency by 25%.
