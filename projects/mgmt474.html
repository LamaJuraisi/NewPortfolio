MGMT 474 - Stock Market Analysis

Exploring stock performance for Nike, Adidas, and Puma

Objective

Analyze the stock performance of Nike, Adidas, and Puma to gain insights into market trends and identify top-performing predictive models for stock price forecasting.

Tools & Techniques

  • R Programming: Used for data cleaning, analysis, and visualization of stock trends.
  • Correlation Analysis: Analyzed relationships between stock prices of Nike, Adidas, and Puma.
  • Random Forest: Achieved an RMSE of 0.60 for predicting Nike's stock prices.
  • Naive Bayes: Tested stock classification accuracy, reaching 45%.
  • K-Nearest Neighbors: Evaluated prediction accuracy with an RMSE of 4.11.

Process Summary

  • Collected stock data for Nike, Adidas, and Puma from reliable sources.
  • Performed regression and correlation analyses to identify patterns and trends.
  • Evaluated predictive models, including Random Forest and Naive Bayes.

Project Insights

The Random Forest model outperformed others with the lowest RMSE, making it ideal for stock price prediction. High correlations (0.88–0.93) among Nike, Adidas, and Puma stocks support portfolio diversification strategies.
