MGMT 474 - Stock Market Analysis

Exploring stock performance for Nike, Adidas, and Puma

Objective

Analyze the stock performance of Nike, Adidas, and Puma to gain insights into market trends and identify top-performing predictive models for stock price forecasting.

Background Information

This project focused on evaluating key stock metrics, including share price, revenue, and profit, to provide actionable insights for portfolio management and financial decision-making.

Process Summary

  • Collected stock data for Nike, Adidas, and Puma from reliable market sources.
  • Performed regression and correlation analyses to identify patterns and trends.
  • Evaluated predictive models, including Random Forest, Naive Bayes, and K-Nearest Neighbors.

Key Insights

Stock Performance

Nike demonstrated consistent growth and market dominance, supported by strong brand recognition and innovation.

Model Performance

Random Forest emerged as the top-performing predictive model for forecasting stock prices.
