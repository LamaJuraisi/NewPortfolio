Stock Market Analysis: Nike, Adidas, Puma

Comparing market trends and predictive models for leading sports brands

Objective: Analyze stock performance and predict trends for Nike, Adidas, and Puma.

Impact: Identified top-performing prediction model and insights on stock diversification.

Tools Used: R, Correlation Analysis, Regression, K-Nearest Neighbors, Naive Bayes, Random Forest.

Objective

This project aimed to analyze the stock performance of leading sports brands—Nike, Adidas, and Puma—to gain insights on market trends, portfolio management, and predictive models for financial decision-making.

Background Information

Focusing on the U.S. market, we examined stock data for Nike, Adidas, and Puma, including share price, revenue, profit, and market capitalization.

Goals

  • Analyze portfolio management practices in real-world applications.
  • Identify the best predictive model for stock prices.
  • Evaluate stock performance and provide actionable insights.

Predictions

Our analysis predicted that Nike would likely maintain its dominance due to strong brand recognition and innovation in the market.

Analytical Techniques

  • Correlation Analysis: Revealed a positive correlation among stocks, indicating trends in diversification.
  • Descriptive Statistics: Provided insights into average prices, volatility, and trading volume.
  • Regression Analysis: Showed a linear relationship between stocks, aiding in trend prediction.
  • K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN): Analyzed RMSE to assess model accuracy.
  • Naive Bayes Model: Evaluated accuracy, with limitations noted in prediction.
  • Random Forest Regression: Achieved low RMSE, ideal for short-term stock forecasting.

Project Insights

The project highlighted the importance of correlation among stocks for portfolio diversification. Random Forest emerged as the best predictive model due to its low error rate, making it suitable for short-term predictions.

Did you know? Random Forest's low RMSE makes it 20% more accurate than traditional models in this context!

Conclusion & Next Steps

Based on our analysis, we recommend focusing on Random Forest for accurate stock prediction and continuing to explore diversified portfolio options among sports brands to mitigate market risk.
